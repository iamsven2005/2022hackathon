Breakfast - Fried Egg Sandwich

Step 1 Melt butter over medium-high heat in a large skillet. Crack eggs into the pan and cook to desired firmness. Just before eggs are cooked, place a slice of cheese over each egg; continue to cook until cheese has melted. Step 2 Transfer each egg to a toasted slice of bread. Season eggs with salt and pepper. Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on remaining slices of bread and cover eggs with bread to make 4 sandwiches. Serve warm.

Lunch - Salmon Salad

Step 1 - Mince shallot and zest lemon. Step 2 - In a small bowl mix lemon zest, lemon juice, shallot, and dijon mustard. Whisk in olive oil until smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste and set aside. Step 3 - Wash and chop lettuce and tomatoes. Set aside. Step 4 - Cook bacon until crispy and let cool. Once cooled, chop into bite-sized pieces. Step 5 - Peel and cube avocado. Step 6 - Divide ingredients into four portions and toss together lettuce, tomato, bacon, and avocado. Step 7 - Pan sear or warm salmon in the oven for a few minutes. Step 8 - Toss salad greens in dressing and top with warmed salmon.


Dinner - Loaded Mediterranean Beef Bowl

Step 1 - Make the greek yogurt sauce by combining ingredients in a bowl. Season with salt and taste. Adjust as needed. Transfer to a squeeze bottle if you have one. It will make the plating that much prettier. Step 2 - Slice cucumbers into half-moons by slicing cut lengthwise and scooping all seeds out with a spoon. Discard seeds. Slice into even half-moons and transfer to a bowl. You can use a mandolin to make this process faster if you have one. Mix Trifecta cherry tomatoes with the cucumbers in the same bowl. Step 3 - Season cucumbers-tomato mix with a splash of lime juice, olive oil, and salt. Taste for seasoning and adjust as needed. Keep on the side until plating time Step 4 - Microwave the Trifecta beef patties covered completely by a wet paper cloth towel, for a minute and a half. After the patties are slightly cooled down, crumble the patties to its original form - ground beef - with your hands into a bowl. Keep warm. Step 5 - Warm-up quinoa in the microwave. In four bowls plate ⅓ cup of quinoa, followed by 4 ounces ground beef, ½ cup seasoned cucumber-tomato mix on the side, 1 tablespoon of hummus on another side, and top with greek yogurt by making long zig zags with a squeeze bottle, or simply spooning 1 tablespoon in the middle of the bowl. Make it as pretty as you’d like! Step 6 - Garnish with red pepper flakes, parsley leaves, or fresh dill.

Breakfast - Chocolate-Raspberry Oatmeal

Step 1 - In a medium saucepan stir together oats, cocoa powder and salt. Stir in almond milk. Step 2 - Bring to boiling over medium heat, stirring occasionally; reduce heat. Step 3 - Simmer, uncovered, 5 to 7 minutes or until thickened, stirring occasionally. Step 4 - Cover. Remove from heat; let stand 2 minutes. Step 5 - Divide oatmeal mixture among four serving bowls. Step 6 - Top each serving with 1/4 cup of the raspberries. If desired, drizzle each serving with 1 teaspoon of the chocolate syrup.


Lunch - Ginger chicken & green bean noodles

Step 1 - Heat the oil in a wok over a high heat and stir-fry the chicken for 5 mins. Step 2 - Add the green beans and stir-fry for 4-5 mins more until the green beans are just tender, and the chicken is just cooked through. Step 3 - Stir in the fresh ginger and garlic, and stir-fry for 2 mins, then add the stem ginger and syrup, the cornflour mix, soy sauce and vinegar. Step 4 - Stir-fry for 1 min, then toss in the noodles. Step 5 - Cook until everything is hot and the sauce coats the noodles. Step 6 - Drizzle with more soy, if you like, and serve.


Dinner - 3-Ingredient Salmon

Step 1 - Place salmon a shallow pan and squeeze lemon juice over pieces. Make sure each piece is coated. Step 2 - Let marinate in fridge for 1 hour (optional but if you have the time let it marinate) Step 3 - Preheat oven to 260 degrees C. Step 4 - Place salmon pieces, skin side down on a parchment lined baking sheet or pan. Step 5 - Cover each piece with italian seasoning and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Step 6 - Bake for 10 mins. Step 7 - Serve immediately.

Breakfast - Banana Bread French Toast

Step 1 - Whisk eggs, milk, vanilla and cinnamon in a 4-cup measuring cup or mixing bowl. Step 2 - Arrange 4 slices of banana bread in a 9-by-13-inch baking dish. Step 3 - Pour half of the egg mixture over the bread. Let soak for 1 minute, then turn the slices over and soak for 1 minute more. Step 4 - Heat 1 tablespoon butter in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the slices and cook until golden, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Step 5 - Transfer to a plate and cover to keep warm. Repeat with the remaining banana bread, egg mixture and butter. Step 6 - To serve, top with walnuts, sliced banana and maple syrup, if desired.


Lunch - Instant Pot Beef Curry

Step 1 - Press Sauté on multicooker and heat 1 tablespoon oil. Add onion and pepper, season with 1/4 teaspoon each salt and pepper, and sauté until just tender, 5 minutes; transfer to bowl. Step 2 - Add remaining tablespoon oil, season beef with 1/4 teaspoon each salt and pepper, and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes. Step 3 - Whisk together coconut milk and curry paste, add to pot, and scrape up any brown bits. Cover and lock lid and cook on high pressure for 35 minutes. Step 4 - Use natural release method for 10 minutes, then release any remaining pressure. Step 5 - Using 2 forks, break up meat into pieces, then toss with onion and red pepper. Step 6 - Serve over rice along with lime wedges and topped with cilantro and red chile if desired.


Dinner - Creamy Chicken & Mushrooms

Step 1 - Sprinkle chicken with 1/4 teaspoon each kosher salt and pepper. Heat 1 tablespoon canola oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Step 2 - Cook the chicken, turning once, until browned and just cooked through, 7 to 10 minutes total. Transfer to a plate. Step 3 - Add 1 tablespoon oil and mushrooms to the pan; cook, stirring occasionally, until the liquid has evaporated, about 4 minutes. Step 4 - Increase heat to high, add wine and cook until it has mostly evaporated, about 4 minutes. Step 5 - Reduce heat to medium; stir in cream, any accumulated juice from the chicken and 1/4 teaspoon each salt and pepper. Step 6 - Return the chicken to the pan and turn to coat with the sauce. Step 7 - Serve the chicken topped with the sauce and sprinkled with parsley.
