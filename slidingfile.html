Ideation

For this hackathon, we have started with the concept of combating climate change in mind. We brainstormed an idea that can be prototype and can be in relatively quick time, yet have the greatest technological inpact to solve climate change, directly from the source.

Information

Approximately 17.3 billion metric tons of greenhouse gases from food production, that is 35% of the worlds greenhouse emmissions. Apps and Ai technology are the fastest implementable solutions that solves problems from the root. As such fast implementable solutions are the key to reducing and combacting climate change.

Implementation

Our Ai crawls through data bases and search engines to accurately predict and calculate the amount of carbon footprint made in a meal. It has the sole goal of reducing carbon footprint of the food industry. It would help to create a healthier lifestyle for uses, reducing even more carbon.

Iteration

In the future if all 8 billion of us follows the meal plans proposed by the AI, climate change can be reduced by up to 40% in farming, storing transporting and producing.
